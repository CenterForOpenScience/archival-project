Helpful Hints
How do I make sure a behavioral health provider accepts my insurance plan?

Log in to the "Member Log In" section to see the behavioral health providers in your plan's network.

What's the Zagat® Health Survey and why do I need to log in to it?

The Zagat® Health Survey lets you share information about a provider. You can write reviews about about a behavioral health provider, hospital, laboratory and other health care facility. Like the restaurant guide, Zagat compiles all reviews and ranks behavioral health providers based on feedback. You can also see what other people said about a provider before you decide to become a patient. You must log in to Zagat to write a review.

How do I compare behavioral health providers?

You can compare up to four behavioral health providers, side by side. Click the "Compare" box by each behavioral health provider and then click the "Compare Now" button.

Can I find these behavioral health providers on a map?

Yes. Click the "View Map" button to see a map.

How do I view the next page of results?

At the top of the screen, you'll see the number of pages you have in your Search Results. Click the "Next" link to move to the next page. Or, enter a page number in the field.

How do I print these results?

Click the "Print Results" link on the left side of the screen.

How do I email my search results to myself or someone else?

Click the "Email" link on the top right of the screen. Then, enter the email address of the person you'd like to send the search results to, as well as your email address. You'll also type out the security code you see on the screen. Then, click the "Send" button.

How do I fax my search results to myself or someone else?

Click the "Fax" link on the top right of the screen. Then, enter the name and fax number of the person you'd like to send the search results to. You'll also enter your name. Then, click the "Send" button.

How do I learn more about a certain behavioral health provider?

Click the behavioral health provider's name to see more information about the behavioral health provider.

How often do you update this information?

The information is updated every week. Click here to see how we make sure our information is correct.
